title: "Basic Emergency Kit"
weight: 10
---

Every household should have these essential items ready:

## Water and Food

- Water: 3 litres per person per day for at least 3 days
- Non-perishable food for at least 3 days
- Manual can opener
- Eating utensils

## Safety and First Aid

- First aid kit
- Flashlight and extra batteries (AA/AAA), USB power banks (charged)
- Battery-powered or hand-crank radio
- Whistle to signal for help

## Documents and Communication

- Copies of important documents in waterproof container
- Mobile phone with chargers, cables and backup battery
- Printed and laminated emergency contact list

## Personal Items

- Prescription medications (at least a week's supply)
- Glasses or contact lenses
- Infant formula and nappies (if needed)
- Pet food and supplies (if needed)

## Tools

- Multi-purpose tool
- Wrench or pliers to turn off utilities
- Local maps
- Dust masks and plastic sheeting

## Additional Recommendations

- Cash in small notes
- Change of clothes and sturdy shoes
- Blankets or sleeping bags
- Sanitation supplies (garbage bags, wet wipes)

**This is an emergency. Leave immediately.**

## Affected Areas

- North Ridge suburb
- Valley View estate
- Hillside Drive and surrounding streets

## What To Do Now

1. Leave immediately via the designated evacuation route
2. Take only essential items (medications, documents, phone)
3. Do not attempt to defend your property
4. Head to the evacuation centre at Community Hall

## Evacuation Route

Travel south on Main Road. Do not use Valley Highway - it is closed.

A flood watch has been issued for the region until Friday.

## Expected Conditions

- 100-150mm of rainfall over the next 48 hours
- Rivers expected to rise significantly
- Flash flooding possible in low-lying areas

## What You Should Do

1. Monitor conditions via this website and local radio
2. Prepare your emergency kit
3. Know your evacuation route
4. Move vehicles and valuables to higher ground
5. Stay away from waterways and stormwater drains

## Areas at Risk

- Creek Road precinct
- Riverside Drive
- Industrial area near the river

## If Flooding Occurs

- Never drive, walk, or swim through floodwater
- Move to higher ground immediately if water rises
- Call 000 if in immediate danger

We will provide updates as the situation develops.
